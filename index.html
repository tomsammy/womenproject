Meet Our Founder

Rev. Ijeoma Eteng Emeribe

"I am Ijeoma Emeribe. Our mission is to empower and restore dignity to vulnerable and displaced women across Africa by connecting them to sustainable job opportunities in partnership with international employers."

A woman of vision, an advocate for vulnerable women and children, and a change agent. Rev. Ijeoma Emeribe leads Woman Africa International with a calling to empower women globally.

365 Days of Activism Against GBV

Moving beyond the standard 16 days, we are creating sustainable SAFE spaces and driving the adoption of policy frameworks to end Gender-Based Violence in Nigeria and Africa.

How We Drive Global Impact

Woman Africa Foundation

Humanitarian Impact

Focused on identifying, sheltering, and rehabilitating vulnerable and displaced women affected by conflict. We provide skills development, trauma counseling, and our dedicated SAFE space for survivors of Gender-Based Violence.

Woman Africa Staffing

Economic Empowerment

The business and economic arm of our mission. It handles employment contracts, international workforce partnerships, and places fully rehabilitated women into safe, dignified, and sustainable jobs locally and abroad.
